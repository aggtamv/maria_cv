Profile

My name is Maria Tamvaki and I have a BA in Sociology (2021) University of Crete, School of Social Studies- Department of Sociology and soon I will have concluded my studies as a post-graduate student in cultural sociology (MSc) at the University of Crete (September 2023) I got my BA Sociology Degree with a final grade of 9.12 excellence, graduating top of my class in June 2021; on top of that Ι scored first in the introductory exams of my master studies in September 2021, while my current grade average is 9.67.
I have participated as a researcher in five different research projects, all of which were mainly focused on matters of social policy, gender issues, social despoilment and destitution. I have gotten an academic achievement scholarship from the University of Crete regarding my Master studies, as well as two more grants from the University of Crete: one so I could work at the Gender Laboratory of the University of Crete and one concerning the research project entitled “Needs Assessment and Study of Local value System in the Agriculture Area of Oropedio Lasithi, Crete” under the supervision of Mr. Zaimakis Ioannis.
Furthermore, I recently presented at the 26th Pan-Hellenic Master Conference for young researchers and PhD candidates of the Sociology department a survey entitled “Methodological research issues in social sciences” which was part of my thesis “Invisible Feminist Collectives: Gender Aspects of Greek Political Graffiti”. Concerning my thesis, I am conducting a qualitative research employing the ethnographic methods and semiotic analysis, whereas part of the research also includes thematic analysis of interviews with members of collectivities who create gendered political graffiti. Finally, I was a Co-Founder of the 1st student radio station of the University of Crete in Rethymno, called Matzore 89.1 FM.
